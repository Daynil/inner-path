Shelley’s Training Expertise

I have been conducting trainings for over 10 years. For 5 years, I was an adjunct professor at Oakton Community College where I taught a course I developed called Sexual Assault Crisis Intervention. Most recently I provided a CEU seminar for the Family Services Counseling Center in Aurora. I have provided trainings in various settings and to various professionals: social service/mental health, law enforcement, medical professionals, and educators. I am adept at tailoring my presentations to fit the needs of the audience.

I can provide trainings or interactive workshops on the following topics:

  • Privilege and Oppression
  • History and Politics of the Rape Crisis Movement
  • Rape Culture
  • Rape Trauma Syndrome and How to Respond to Sexual Assault Survivors
  • Crisis Intervention with Adult Survivors of Sexual Assault
  • Crisis Intervention with Child and Adolescent Survivors of Sexual Assault
  • Sexual Violence
  • Medical Advocacy with Sexual Assault Survivors
  • Legal Advocacy with Sexual Assault Survivors
  • Vicarious Trauma and Self-Care
  • Animal Assisted Therapy

If you are interested in having me provide trainings or workshops for your agency or staff, feel free to contact me!
