Reiki

Reiki (pronounced ray-key) is a Japanese energy technique that means "universal life energy" and is used for stress reduction and relaxation. The hands channel this energy to help clear any blockages from our minds, hearts, and bodies in order to promote healing. This activates the healing processes of the body and restores well-being.

Reiki helps to rebalance your energy bringing your mental, emotional, and physical self back into balance. Blockages are also released to help you live a life of mindfulness.
