Art Therapy

Art therapy is a form of expressive therapy that uses the creative process of making art to improve a child’s, teen’s, or adult’s physical, mental, and emotional well being. Art Therapy blends the fields of human development, visual arts, and the creative process with models of counseling and psychotherapy. Self-expression of your true being comes forth in your creations. No art experience is necessary; only a willingness to bring light to the unknown parts of yourself.

"Art can permeate the very deepest part of us, where no words exist."

~ Eileen Miller
