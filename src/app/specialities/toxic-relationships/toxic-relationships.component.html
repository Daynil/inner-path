Healing from Toxic Relationships

Relationships are never easy. There are many reasons a relationship might transition from one status to another. Uncertainty in relationships is often confusing and painful, but you don't have to remain stuck. Our therapist, Eryn, can help you navigate issues related to the following:

  • Questioning Whether to Stay in a Relationship
  • Toxic or Abusive Relationships
  • Breaking-Up/Transitions
  • Betrayal/Infidelity
  • Divorce

We can help you find your way through the emotional, psychological, and financial maze of contentious relationship transitions or divorce and, if applicable, help you develop your "radar" for choosing healthy partners in the future.
