Dialectical Behavior Therapy

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) is a set of behavioral skills that allow you to learn to better handle difficult situations and daily life without becoming emotionally overwhelmed. DBT teaches the important concept of validation along with specific new behaviors within four core skill areas: mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness. DBT skills help us to learn to handle life's discomforts and how to set and maintain boundaries, ask for what we need, and overall improve our effectiveness in our relationships.
