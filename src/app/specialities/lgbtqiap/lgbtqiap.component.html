LGBTQIAP

We are able to provide individual, couples, and family therapy for those who are lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender/transexual, queer/gender variant, questioning, intersex, asexual, and pansexual. As a member of this community, Shelley understands the importance of having a helping professional who respects, supports, and empathizes with you and your journey, as well as understands the complex challenges you may be facing. Inner Path Counseling and Wellness strives to create a safe environment, free from judgement, where you can be yourself and your identity can be supported.

Common areas of focus for individuals in this community may be:

  • Coming out
  • Trans issues such as internal identity, transitioning, transparenting, etc
  • Exploration of gender identities/expressions
  • Exploration of sexual orientations
  • Parenting LGBTQIAP children
  • Facilitating a healthy identity
  • Supporting LGBTQIAP family/friends
  • Social support
  • Creating a chosen family
  • Intimacy and dating
  • HIV/chronic illness
  • Oppression, marginalization, discrimination
  • Trauma
  • Intimate partner and/or family abuse
  • Depression and/or anxiety
  • Religious issues
  • Identifying with preferred pronouns

If you feel you would like to discuss any of these issues, or an issue specific to your situation, know that we are here for you. Feel free to contact us and we look forward to hearing from you!
