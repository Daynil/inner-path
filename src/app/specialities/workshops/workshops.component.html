Workshops/Groups

Reconnecting with the Wild Woman Archetype Group

The book Women Who Run with the Wolves is an amazing resource for those wanting to reconnect with our inner Wild Woman. We meet twice a year and there is often a beginner and advanced group going. This is a powerful meeting of women who are curious and willing to dive deep into reading and discussing archetypal ideas around the feminine and the powerful that resides within each of us. Workshop dates to be announced. Contact for more info.

Creative Healing

Sometimes working through the pain, shame, and hurt of life is easier to express without words. Through collage, markers, beading, and journaling, there are many ways creativity helps us heal. We can help facilitate your creative healing or reconnect you with blocked creativity through vision board making. Workshop dates to be announced. Contact for more info.

What is "Healthy"? Develop Your "Relationship Radar"

In this workshop we explore, as a group, what characterizes a healthy relationship ("green flags") and contrast that with what is unhealthy ("red flags"). By the end of the workshop, you will have a new "radar" for sensing healthy and unhealthy characteristics in relationships along with your own personal list of what you do and don't want in future relationships.
