Statistically, couples who have had pre-marital counseling weather the storms of the first year of marriage better than those who have have not received such counseling. Pre-marital counseling will acquaint the couple with primarily six areas of conflict that most couples face which include money/finance, in-laws, child rearing, sex, roles, and religion/spirituality.

We believe is important to work with newly committed couples on gaining clarity on important topics in a safe and facilitated environment. We also encourage couples to develop a healthy communication and conflict resolution style, which are key to a successful and healthy marriage. Most clients report that pre-marital counseling is a positive, affirming, and bonding experience that enhances their commitment to marriage.
