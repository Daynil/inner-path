IFS/Parts Work

Internal Family Systems (IFS) Therapy is an evidence-based approach for working with trauma and other troubling issues. It helps to get to the root of understanding a variety of unwanted or mysterious behaviors (they are often serving a hidden function for you!)

Read more about IFS therapy and the founder of the theory here.

In IFS, also known as "Parts Work", we discover the "parts of Self" operating behind the scenes and we get to know these hardworking parts. Some parts are working very hard for you and are ignored or unappreciated. Some parts are misunderstood in their ways they try to "help". We have lots of "parts" of Self trying to help us in their own unusual ways. The diagram below explains some of the parts we each have (Manager, Firefighters and Exiles).

"There are no 'bad' parts, the goal of therapy is not to eliminate parts but instead help them find their non-extreme roles." -Dick Schwartz, creator of Internal Family Systems Therapy
