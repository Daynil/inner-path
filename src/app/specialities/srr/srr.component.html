Somatic Resilience and Regulation

Somatic Resilience and Regulation (SRR) is a form of somatic psychotherapy that supports recovery from developmental trauma by improving your resilience and self regulation of your own nervous systems. SRR theory is based on a combination of attachment theory, developmental trauma psychology, somatic psychotherapy, and gentle noninvasive touch. It is not massage therapy, but a somatic approach to trauma recovery.
