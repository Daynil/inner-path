Couples Therapy

While past generations of couples might have taken the attitude of “grin and bear it” when difficulties arise in their relationship, these days most couples are less willing to tolerate an unhappy relationship or marriage. Couples often seek therapy in order to improve romantic relationships, resolve interpersonal conflicts, and improve communication techniques. Timing is everything when it comes to couples counseling – waiting too long to get help can be very costly, in more ways than one.

When seeing a couples therapist, the therapist is more like an experienced consultant rather than a fixer. Couples counseling is a dynamic process and it requires each partner to take an active role, while a therapist assists and guides the couple to consider new and different ways of looking at things. Our couples therapist is trained in Emotion Focused Couples Therapy, the Gottman method, and other solution focused relationship strategies to help clients create real change.
