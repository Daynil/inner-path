Yoga

Yoga is a practical aid and is based on an ancient art that harmonizes the development of the body, mind, and spirit. Breath control, simple meditation, and body postures are practiced for relaxation. Our Yoga trained therapist personalizes sessions to each individual client. Sessions are tailored for unique, sequenced asanas (positions) for clients dealing with anxiety, depression, and sleeplessness, or needing relaxation and the alignment of the chakras. Yoga is a body centered meditation used to clear the mind, connect to the Self, and tap into your power of awareness.
