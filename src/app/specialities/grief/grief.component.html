Grief

Grief happens when you experience feelings as the result of something changing or ending. Most people think of grief when it relates to death of a person, however there are many reasons why you might be experiencing grief. The following is a list of causes for grief, however because grief is a form of trauma, note that your grief is personal to you and might not exactly fit in these categories.
  • Death of a person
  • Death of a pet/animal
  • Divorce/separation
  • Injury/illness
  • Major life changes (for the good or bad)
  • Work changes
  • Family issues/changes
  • Sexual difficulties
  • Marital difficulties
  • Financial changes
  • School difficulties or changes
  • Change in living conditions
  • Loss or changes in friendships
  • Holidays
  • Loss of childhood
  • Loss of safety
  • Identity issues
