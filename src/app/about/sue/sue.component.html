Sue Shekut, LCPC, LMT

Sue Shekut, LCPC, LMT, is a psychotherapist in private practice, specializing in trauma recovery and relationship issues. Sue holds a master's degree in Clinical Professional Psychology from Roosevelt University and is trained in Dialectical Behavior Therapy, EMDR, Somatic Resilience and Regulation, Sex Therapy and Trauma-Sensitive Yoga. Sue is also a Certified Sex Addiction Therapist. In addition, Sue has been a bodyworker and yoga practitioner for over 19 years with a deep understanding of how to calm the nervous system and help people cope with pain and stress. Sue's approach is collaborative, versus confrontational. She integrates wellness, interpersonal neuropsychology, and positive psychology in her therapy sessions. Sue uses the tools of validation, compassion, expressive arts, somatic awareness, and gentle humor to help client heal.
