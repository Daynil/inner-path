Monique Prohaska-Slattery LCPC, ATR, CYT

My passion is to help others find peace, perseverance, and resilience through everyday struggles and stressors.

Whether you are a woman, child, teen, or young adult going through a life transition, a relationship issue, experiencing stress, facing anxiety or depression, or anything else that's letting you know that "something's not quite right," finding a healthy outlet to express yourself and explore solutions is where your transformation begins.

For 20 years I have worked with ages 6 years to adult in special education schools and private practice encouraging a healthy approach to their mind, body, and spirit. I believe that connecting with my clients is the most important part in therapy. Working together in a safe environment encourages change which promotes belief in one's self and capabilities. I use a variety of therapeutic modalities to help clients gain increased self-esteem, positive coping strategies, communication, and healthy relationships.

I am a Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor (LCPC), Registered Art Therapist (ATR), as well as a Certified Yoga Teacher (CYT) and Reiki Master Practitioner. I also use holistic therapies such as yoga, meditation, reiki, and mindfulness techniques into sessions when appropriate.

I look forward to accompanying you on your journey to finding creative solutions to make healing connections!
