Eryn Smith-Moeller, LCPC

When I work with clients, I am helping people take steps towards living the life they dream of; I'm doing what I believe in and what I love.

Psychotherapy can bring insight and powerful transformation to your life.

I love being part of the healing process; empowering people with knowledge and options about how they can grow and change. I am honored to be part of each person's sacred path toward healing.

It is important to get to the root of the problem, not just manage symptoms.

With more than nine years experience as a trauma psychotherapist, one of the gifts I bring is listening deeply and helping you feel understood while helping discover and implement practical solutions.

As a Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor (LCPC) I've worked in a variety of settings: crisis hotlines, community mental health centers, domestic violence shelters, with victims of sexual violence, with college students at 4-year university, and in a half-way house for men exiting chemical dependency programs.

I look forward to helping you reconnect with your intuition in a way that honors your unique path.
